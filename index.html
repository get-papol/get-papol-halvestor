Everything you need to know about halal investing with Halvestor.

Halvestor is a Shariah-compliant investing app built specifically for Muslim investors. We offer a paper trading simulator so you can practice investing with real market data using virtual money, with every stock pre-screened for halal compliance. A full trading platform is coming soon.

We use the AAOIFI (Accounting and Auditing Organisation for Islamic Financial Institutions) methodology to screen stocks. This means we check debt ratios, interest income levels, business activity, and purification requirements across 40,000+ stocks, updated continuously.

Yes — the paper trading simulator is completely free with no subscription fee. Our survey found that 86% of Muslim investors prefer anything other than a subscription model, so we built accordingly. Waitlist members will also receive free access to the full platform when it launches.

Paper trading means practicing with virtual money using real market prices. It lets you learn how markets work, test strategies, and build confidence without risking real capital. For Muslim investors who are new to investing, it is the perfect starting point.

Halvestor monitors your holdings continuously. If a stock in your portfolio fails our Shariah screening criteria — due to a change in the company's business activities or financial ratios — you will receive an immediate compliance alert so you can take action.

Zakat is the annual Islamic obligation to donate 2.5% of qualifying wealth to those in need. Halvestor's built-in Zakat calculator analyses your portfolio and calculates exactly what you owe based on your holdings, so you can fulfil this obligation accurately and with confidence.
